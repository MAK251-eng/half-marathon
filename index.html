⚠️ Injury Guardrails
  • MCL: Stop any run if you feel medial knee pain, swelling, or instability. Two-day rule before returning.
  • Labrum: No flat barbell bench, no overhead barbell press, no dips. DBs for all pressing.
  • Both: Connective tissue adapts slower than muscle. Don't skip Phase 1 intervals even if they feel easy.

~8–10 minutes. Movement-based, not static holds.

🌡️ Sauna Protocol
